GAIA

Gaia is a textured typeface based on the handwritten style. It is inspired by the goddess Gaia from Greek mythology in which she was considered the goddess of fertility and creation.

This font is perfect for products such as menus, posters or prints with a natural and ecological style. You can see the complete Proyect HERE and download it free of charge for commercial or personal use.

On May 25, 2020 Gaia was featured in the "Handwritten" category by Fontdesk.
